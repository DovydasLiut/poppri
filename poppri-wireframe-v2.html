⑧ FAQ / Trust

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your items authentic? +

Yes. Every item is inspected and honestly described. Luxury pieces are authenticated before listing. 99.9% positive feedback across 856K reviews.

How do the auctions work? +

On eBay you browse and bid at your own pace — you only pay if you win. On Whatnot we sell live and you bid in real time. Both offer full buyer protection.

What brands do you sell? +

From everyday labels to Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Gucci and Prada. Around 30% of inventory is high-end designer — sourced from old collections and brand returns.

How is shipping handled? +

Typically dispatched next day. Tracked delivery to USA, UK and Europe. Every order carefully packed — items arrive exactly as described.

What condition are items in? +

Every listing includes a clear condition grade. We sell new-with-tags through to pre-loved. Nothing is ever misrepresented.

Where can I watch live shows? +

Live shows run on Whatnot several times a week. Follow us at whatnot.com/user/poppri_live to get notified the moment we go live.
